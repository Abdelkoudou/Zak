# Brand Identity & Guidelines

**Brand Name:** FMC App

**Core Identity:** The essential study companion for students at the Faculty of Medicine of Constantine.

This skill defines the core constraints for visual design and technical implementation for the FMC App brand. You must adhere to these guidelines strictly to maintain consistency.

### Mission
Making medical revision **effortless, organized, and deeply engaging**.

## Quick Reference: Logo Guidelines

### Primary Logo
- A handwritten "Fmc" wordmark
- **Must not** be modified, stretched, or distorted

### The Wave Mark
- A stylized "m" curve used as a standalone symbol
- Use in compact spaces: app icons, social media avatars

### Logo Usage Hierarchy (Preferred Order)
1. **Black Wave Mark** on Teal (Light Green Sea) background *(Preferred)*
2. **Teal Wave Mark** on Black background
3. **White Wave Mark** on Black background

### Clear Space
- Defined by the letter "C" from the wordmark
- No elements should be within 1x "C" distance of the logo
