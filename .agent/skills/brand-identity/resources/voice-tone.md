# Copywriting: Voice & Tone Guidelines

When generating text for **FMC App**, adhere to this brand persona.

---

## Brand Personality Keywords
* **Elegant** — Premium look and feel in every interaction
* **Professional** — Serious about medical education
* **Comprehensive** — Thorough and complete coverage
* **Approachable** — Welcoming to all students
* **Effortless** — Simple and intuitive experiences

---

## Brand Voice

### Essence
FMC App speaks as a **knowledgeable mentor** — supportive, clear, and focused on helping medical students succeed in their revision.

### Tone Spectrum
| Context | Tone |
| :--- | :--- |
| Success messages | Encouraging, warm |
| Error messages | Clear, helpful, non-blaming |
| Instructions | Direct, concise |
| Marketing copy | Inspiring, confident |
| Educational content | Professional, authoritative |

---

## Grammar & Mechanics Rules

* **Headings:** Use Title Case for main headings (H1, H2). Use sentence case for subheadings (H3+).
* **Punctuation:** Avoid exclamation points (!) in standard interface copy. Use periods for complete sentences.
* **Clarity:** Prefer active voice over passive voice. Keep sentences concise.
* **Numbers:** Spell out numbers one through nine; use numerals for 10 and above.

---

## Terminology Guide

| Do Not Use | Use Instead |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Utilize" | "Use" |
| "In order to..." | "To..." |
| "Quiz" | "QCM" |
| "Test" | "Revision" or "Practice" |
| "Correct/Wrong" | "Correct/Incorrect" |

---

## Key Brand Messages

Use these phrases to maintain brand consistency:

* **Mission tagline:** "Making medical revision effortless, organized, and deeply engaging."
* **Vision statement:** "The definitive revision standard for every medical student."
* **Value prop:** "Your essential study companion at the Faculty of Medicine of Constantine."
